RVTRAX Product Overview

Maintenance tracking, manufacturer encyclopedia, and modification registry for RV owners. A Prop-Trax product line extension by WTE Solutions.

Platform — Three Core Layers

Every user registers their unit by VIN. The platform combines a maintenance vault, a manufacturer knowledge base, and a community modification registry.

Maintenance & Document Vault

Prop-Trax logic applied to rolling homes. Service events, documents, and recalls tied to your VIN.

  • VIN-anchored unit profile
  • Full maintenance log
  • Document repository
  • NHTSA recall sync
  • Resale health report

RV Knowledge Encyclopedia

Manufacturer-seeded specs for every make, model, year, and floor plan.

  • Factory specs and equipment
  • Known issues and TSBs
  • Recall history
  • Community-enhanced data

Modification Registry

Modifications documented by owners become discoverable by others with the same model.

  • Category taxonomy
  • Part numbers and costs
  • Installation notes
  • Community validation

Go-to-Market Roadmap

18-month phased launch: foundation, community growth, and monetization expansion.

Phase 1 · Months 1–4

Foundation

Build core Vault and seed encyclopedia for top 50 RV models. Soft launch with beta group from iRV2, RV LIFE, and RV Mod Squad.

Phase 2 · Months 5–9

Community Growth

Open modification registry and affiliate commerce. Content marketing for long-tail maintenance and common-problems SEO.

Phase 3 · Months 10–18

Monetization Expansion

Dealer/Fleet tier, insurance and manufacturer partnerships, native mobile apps, white-label licensing.
